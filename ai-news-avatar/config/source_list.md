# Source List

## Priority 1 - Primary Sources
- OpenAI blog and releases
- Anthropic news and releases
- Google AI / DeepMind announcements
- xAI announcements
- Meta AI announcements
- Mistral announcements
- Hugging Face product updates
- major inference/platform release blogs

## Priority 2 - Reliable Secondary Sources
- official engineering blogs
- selected tech media with timestamps and source citations

## Content Focus
Only keep stories about:
- new models
- model upgrades
- inference engines
- APIs
- multimodal tools
- developer platform launches
- important pricing or capability changes

## Ignore
- generic market commentary
- funding news without product relevance
- opinion pieces
- low-signal rumor threads
