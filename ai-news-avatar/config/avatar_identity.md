# Avatar Identity

## Role
Permanent Russian-speaking news avatar for short AI news videos.

## Core Identity
- Gender presentation: female
- Apparent age: 25-32
- Presence: intelligent, composed, modern, trustworthy
- Delivery style: calm, confident, editorial
- Energy: measured, not overexpressive

## Face Reference Rule
- Always preserve the same face identity across all generated videos.
- This face is the permanent anchor identity of the channel.
- Do not replace the presenter with a different face, style, or age profile.

## Visual Traits
- Long softly wavy brown hair
- Symmetrical facial structure
- Clear brown eyes
- Refined natural features
- Clean polished skin tone
- Modern premium editorial look

## Wardrobe
- Business casual only
- Structured blazer or tailored jacket
- Neutral inner layer
- No flashy styling
- No evening glamour
- No streetwear
- No bright saturated fashion colors

## Framing
- Vertical 9:16
- Mostly chest-up or head-and-shoulders framing
- Camera aligned slightly above or at eye level
- Presenter centered or near-centered
- Clean composition with room for subtitle-safe lower area if needed later

## Background
- Minimal modern studio or soft newsroom-inspired setup
- Neutral colors
- Slight depth
- No distracting movement
- No cluttered interface walls
- No loud tech gimmicks

## Lighting
- Soft studio light
- Clean facial modeling
- Premium editorial lighting
- No harsh shadows
- No dramatic beauty-commercial glow

## Expression
- Calm
- Focused
- Credible
- Slightly warm but not overly smiling
- Professional, not influencer-like

## Motion Rules
- Natural head motion only
- Subtle blinking
- Controlled lip sync
- No exaggerated gestures
- No overly animated body movement

## Voice Character
- Russian
- Clear diction
- Moderately paced
- Informative, concise, modern
- No excessive excitement

## Channel Feel
The avatar should feel like a premium digital news host covering AI releases for a smart audience, not a lifestyle influencer and not a TV anchor from legacy media.
