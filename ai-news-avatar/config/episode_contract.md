# Episode Contract

## Objective
Each episode is a 30-second Russian AI news short delivered by the permanent talking avatar.

## Runtime
- Target: 30 seconds
- Acceptable range: 25-35 seconds

## Language
- Russian only

## Topic Scope
Only cover:
- new AI models
- model updates
- inference engines
- AI APIs
- multimodal launches
- significant product releases in the AI tooling ecosystem

Do not cover:
- generic startup news
- pure investment news without product significance
- vague rumors
- opinion-only stories

## Episode Structure
1. Hook
2. Main update
3. Why it matters
4. Brief close

## Script Rules
- Clear spoken Russian
- One news item per episode
- No long background explanations
- No jargon overload without payoff
- No fake urgency
- No unsupported claims

## Host Rules
- Use the permanent avatar identity
- Business casual presentation
- Calm authoritative delivery
- Keep motion minimal and consistent

## Review Package Requirements
Before approval, every episode must include:
- selected story headline
- source links
- Russian factual brief
- final Russian script
- title draft
- description draft
- hashtags
- avatar video preview link or file

## Publish Rule
No auto-publish without explicit approval.

## Quality Check
An episode is publishable only if:
- the story is recent and relevant
- the script is understandable in one listen
- the tone is factual and modern
- the avatar output is visually consistent with channel identity
