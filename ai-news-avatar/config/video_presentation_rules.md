# Video Presentation Rules

## Format
- Platform target: YouTube Shorts
- Aspect ratio: 9:16
- Duration target: 30 seconds
- Language: Russian

## Presenter Setup
- Use the permanent avatar identity
- Business casual wardrobe
- Stable visual identity across episodes

## Delivery Style
- Short, factual, fast to understand
- Clean and modern tone
- Strong hook in first 2-3 seconds
- No hype without substance
- No clutter

## Background Style
- Clean modern editorial background
- Soft neutral depth
- Premium but understated

## Screen Graphics
- Keep minimal
- For MVP, avoid overlays entirely if they reduce quality
- Never overload the frame with UI

## Editing Style
- Minimal cuts
- Clean talking-head presentation
- Optional subtle motion background
- Optional product/model visual inserts in future versions

## What To Avoid
- Loud thumbnails inside the video
- Sensationalist gestures
- Hyperactive pacing
- Meme-style edits
- Overly glossy influencer energy
