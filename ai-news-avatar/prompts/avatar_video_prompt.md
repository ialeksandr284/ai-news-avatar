# Avatar Video Prompt

Use this prompt when generating the talking-avatar visual setup for the AI news host.

## Base Prompt
Create a vertical 9:16 talking-avatar news video featuring the same permanent female presenter identity used by this channel. She has long softly wavy brown hair, clear brown eyes, refined natural facial features, and a calm intelligent presence. Dress her in business casual: a tailored blazer with a neutral top. Frame her chest-up in a clean modern editorial studio environment with soft premium lighting and a minimal background. Her delivery should feel composed, credible, modern, and concise, suitable for Russian AI news. No flashy graphics, no influencer energy, no cluttered interface elements.

## Identity Rules
- same face every episode
- business casual wardrobe
- premium clean lighting
- neutral modern background
- subtle natural motion only

## Motion Rules
- natural blinking
- subtle head movement
- realistic lip sync
- no exaggerated expression

## Quality Rules
- the presenter must feel consistent across episodes
- avoid changing age, face shape, hair color, or styling dramatically
- output should feel like a premium digital AI news host
